Open Electives

Welcome!


3rd year students can now fill in their choices for the open elective courses offered to them in the 6th semester. Unlike the first come first serve basis, students will be preferred for a particular course considering their CGPIs.
Students who fail to fill in their choices within deadline will be enrolled in any course RANDOMLY .

You will be given course descriptions of various courses and you can choose accordingly.
NOTE: You will be required to enter the password which you can get from the offices of your respective departments.
